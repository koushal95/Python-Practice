Merge Strings:

Merge two strings with characters alternated from each string at a time. If the strings are of unequal length, append the remaining characters at the end.

A: 'abc', B: 'def' --> result: 'adbecf'
A: 'abcpqr', B: 'def' --> result: 'adbecfpqr'
A: 'abc', B: 'defpqr' --> result: 'adbecfpqr'
